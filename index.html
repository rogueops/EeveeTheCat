Remembering Eevee

The Sweet, Stubborn, and Sassy Queen of the House

About Eevee

Eevee was a blacked-out version of the "Grumpy Cat" with a heart full of love and a personality bigger than her tiny frame. Adopted at the age of 6 after a tough start in life, she quickly became the little queen of her new home. She loved sunbathing, gourmet treats, and ruling her kingdom with an iron paw. Eevee lived to be 16 years old and left behind a legacy of love, sass, and unforgettable memories.

Eevee's Quirks

  • She was super picky about her food but loved gourmet treats.
  • She thought she ran the house (and she did).
  • She had a stubborn streak but was incredibly sweet.
  • She loved sunbathing and would always find the sunniest spot in the house.

A Tribute to Eevee

Eevee was more than just a cat; she was family. Her sassy attitude and loving nature made her unforgettable. She may be gone, but her memory lives on in the hearts of everyone who knew her. Rest in peace, little queen.
